Certificate Verification

Enter the unique certificate code printed on your ML Club, UU certificate to instantly verify its authenticity and download a copy.

The code is printed at the bottom of your certificate. Example: MLUU-2026-001 or simply the filename without extension.

📜

Your verified certificate will appear here.

How it works
1

Find your code

Look at the bottom or back of your ML Club certificate for the unique code.

2

Type & search

Enter the code in the search box above and click Verify.

3

View & download

Your certificate is displayed. Click Download to save a copy.
