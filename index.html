Why Join Us?

Everything we offer

Whether you're just starting out or already coding neural networks — ML Club, UU has something for you.

🧠

Structured Learning

Weekly workshops and study sessions — from Python basics to deep learning, guided by senior members and faculty.

💻

Hands-On Projects

Work on real datasets, build portfolio projects, and solve problems that matter — not just textbook exercises.

🏆

Competitions

Participate in inter-university hackathons and Kaggle competitions as a team, with club support and mentorship.

🎙️

Guest Lectures

Learn from industry professionals and researchers from Bangladesh's top tech companies and AI labs.

🤝

Peer Community

Study together, debug together, grow together. A supportive community of UU students who love AI.

📜

Certificates & Recognition

Earn workshop completion certificates and club recognition that strengthen your resume and career prospects.
