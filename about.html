About ML Club, UU

A student-led community at Uttara University dedicated to AI and Machine Learning education.

Our Mission

AI Education for Every UU Student

Machine Learning Club, Uttara University was founded with a simple belief: every student at UU — regardless of department — deserves access to quality AI education and a community that supports their growth.

We run free workshops, host hackathons, publish learning resources, and connect students with real-world ML opportunities. No fees, no barriers — just curiosity and community.

🎓

A home for 320+ students from CSE, EEE, BBA, and every department at Uttara University — all united by curiosity about AI.

Our Story

How we started

Early 2023

Founded by a handful of curious students

A group of 8 CSE students started a WhatsApp group to share ML resources and help each other learn Python and data science.

Mid 2023

First official workshop — 30 attendees

Organized the first formal Python for ML workshop. The overwhelming response showed there was a real need at UU.

2024

First Hackathon — 12 teams, 50+ participants

Hosted UU's first ever ML hackathon. The winning team built a crop disease detector that got featured in local media.

2025

320+ members, this website, and growing

Now one of UU's most active student clubs — with a full curriculum, regular events, and members from every department.
