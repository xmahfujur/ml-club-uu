About ML Club, UU

A student-led community at Uttara University dedicated to AI and Machine Learning education.

Our Mission

AI Education for Every UU Student

Machine Learning Club, Uttara University was founded with a simple belief: every student at UU regardless of department deserves access to quality AI education and a community that supports their growth.

We run free workshops, host hackathons, publish learning resources, and connect students with real world ML opportunities. No fees, no barriers just curiosity and community.

Join Us Free →

A home for 1000+ students from CSE, EEE and every department at Uttara University all united by curiosity about AI.

Values

What we stand for

Open Access


All workshops and resources are free. Knowledge should have no barrier for UU students.

Community First


We grow together. Senior members mentor newcomers, no one is left behind.

Hands-On Learning


We build real things. Every workshop ends with code you wrote yourself.

Local Impact


We apply ML to solve real problems in Bangladesh our hackathons are proof.
