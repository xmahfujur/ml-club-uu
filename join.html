FAQ

Common questions

Who can join ML Club, UU? +
Any currently enrolled student at Uttara University can join, regardless of department or semester. We welcome students from CSE, EEE, BBA, and all other disciplines.
Do I need prior coding experience? +
Not at all! Our beginner workshops start from scratch — Python basics, math fundamentals, everything. Just bring your curiosity.
Is there a membership fee? +
Membership is completely free for all UU students. Some special events or printed materials may have a small nominal cost, but joining the club is always free.
How often do you hold workshops? +
We typically hold 1–2 workshops or sessions per week. You'll get notifications via our group chat and this website's Events page.
Can I get a certificate for workshops? +
Yes! We issue digital certificates for completed workshop series and major events. These can be added to your CV and LinkedIn profile.
